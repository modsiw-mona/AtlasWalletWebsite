The Atlas Wallet is a Digital Wallet created by the Unsere Kleine company in late 2020. Our compromise with customers is adapting to the digital era, by transitioning from regular credit cards and banking systems, to the online revolution involving the e-commerce world and keeping up with user requirements in the digital landscape by providing alternate payment methods.

What is a digital wallet?

A digital wallet is a sofware-based online service that allows people or businesses to make financial exchanges electronically. Also known as an e-wallet, it stores the users' payment information accross different websites. When working along with mobile payment system, it enables the user to buy anything quicker using your smartphone or smartwatch and by storing your prefered payment settings.
